02 — Experimental New Media

Frank &
Somewhere Island

An interactive sci-fi visual story — written entirely by me, with ChatGPT as an editing tool — released episodically on Discord. The audience was split into rival factions following parallel timelines; community votes shaped the plot after each episode.

The visual pipeline was genuinely hybrid: AI generation (Midjourney, video tools, After Effects) combined with handmade animation from collaborating animators, plus a 3D body scan that physically grounds the character in the real world. The result lived somewhere between cinema, gaming, and community art.

The story culminated in 222 Bitcoin ordinals on Magic Eden. The full collection sold out in three minutes. Beyond the collection, what excited me most was the community that formed: fan theories, meme channels, members posting their own Frank artwork — a living world around the story.
